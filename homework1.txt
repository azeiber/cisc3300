5. What are the parts of a CSS rule?

6. Explain how CSS rules cascade. 
When many rules apply to the same element, the more specific rule will take precedence. Also, if there is a rule that applies to the parent, it will apply to the child as well unless otherwise specified due to inheritance. Finally, the last rule will often take precedence over styles declared earlier.
